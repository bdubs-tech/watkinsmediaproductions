Many Types of Bees

There are around 20,000 species of bees, but only a few of these are honey bees. Some of the honey bees available for purchase in the United States include:

  • Italian
  • Carniolan
  • Caucasian
  • German/English
  • Russian
  • Buckfast
  • Cordovan
